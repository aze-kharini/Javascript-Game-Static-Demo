Instructions

Controls

  • Move either with w, a, s, d or arrows
  • Attack and look around with your mouse
  • Roll with a spacebar or with middle click
  • Menu can be accessed by pressing the letter m

GUI

  • Health is shown in the top left corner
  • Final Boss Distance is shown below your health, the smaller it is, the closer he is
  • Compass pointing to the position of the next boss is shown around the character
  • Number of Spells can be found out by looking at the staff, blue means you have spells left, once it turns white, you have waster all of them

Game Mechanics

  • Score is simply the time it took you to finnish the game.
  • Damage is proportional to your speed, so for example if you hit an enemy from a roll, it will deal way more damage
  • Rolling makes you invincible while rolling
  • Levels can be increased by beating a boss, you have to beat all of them to get on a level of the final boss, Do not try to fight a boss or an enemy that the compass is not pointing to, they are way stronger
  • Final Boss spawns after you beat the Fungiant right in the place where you spawned, he will get progressively faster with each defeated boss and follow you around the map the whole time, wou win after you beat him
  • Mushrooms will heal you if you hit them, red ones are better than brown ones
  • Campfires will recharge your spells after you hit them
  • Boulders rolled by the Golem boss will follow you, the only way to get rid off them is to roll through them, or make them hit someone else.

Tips and Tricks

  • Do NOT stand in the same place while being hit, much better is to roll away
  • If your health gets too low, roll away and collect mushrooms
  • First Isolate the boss from his minions, before fighting with him
